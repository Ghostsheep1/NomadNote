NomadNote Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

NomadNote is designed as a local-first, no-account travel planner. The app does not require a login, email address, phone number, or mandatory backend account.

Data Storage

Trip data, places, notes, itineraries, packing lists, reservations, and journal entries are stored locally on your device by default using browser storage or the app's local web storage container.

Data Collection

The app owner does not collect personal data through NomadNote. NomadNote does not include advertising tracking or analytics tracking by default.

Network Requests

Map tiles and optional geocoding/search features may contact public map providers. These providers may receive technical information such as your IP address and the map/search request needed to provide the feature.

Export and Import

You can export trip data as JSON. Exported files are controlled by you. Be careful when sharing exported trip files because they may contain personal travel plans.

Deleting Data

You can clear local data from the app settings or by deleting the app/browser storage from your device.

Contact

For support, use the support link provided on the app listing or GitHub repository.
